About Me

I am a physical oceanographer and ocean modeller. Currently, I am working as a Project Scientist-II at the National Centre for Medium Range Weather Forecasting (NCMRWF), Noida, which comes under the Ministry of Earth Sciences, Govt. of India. I have completed my PhD from Centre for Atmospheric Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi.My primary research area is mesoscale oceanic eddies of the Bay of Bengal , which I have explored using the ROMS and the COAWST numerical model.
